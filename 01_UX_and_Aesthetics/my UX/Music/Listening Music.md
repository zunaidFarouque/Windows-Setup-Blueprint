# Lyrics Management

I use ChordPro files for managing lyrics.

**PC:**

- Linkesoft Songbook (Windows)

**Android:**

- Songbook Pro

To keep my lyrics organized and backed up, I save and sync them online in Songbook Pro. When transferring files from my PC, I open Songbook Pro Manager, which creates a local Wi-Fi tunnel. I then drag and drop the lyrics files from Linkesoft Songbook (Windows) into the app. Once the upload is complete, I sync my data to Google Drive. Occasionally, I perform a full local backup for extra security.
